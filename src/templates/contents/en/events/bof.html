What is BoF?

Birds of a feather flock together

According to Wikipedia, BoF comes from a motto “Birds of a feather flock together.” A BoF session means an informal meeting at conferences, where the attendees group together based on a shared interest and carry out discussions without any pre-planned agenda.

Do you want to share ideas and creative projects with PyCon TW? Everyone is welcome to the PyConTW 2016 BoF, let us implement the future, together!

Any Preparation?

In BoF, any topic related to Python, PyCon is appreciated, feel free to raise your own topic. Besides, an abundant supply of food is provided!

You can register as a host, bring new or funny projects: fighting robots, LED control system, lighting, sound system and etc. If you find no time to prepare a project, you are also welcome to sing a song or play guitar.

It is also great to just be an audience. Seize this opportunity to make friends, broaden your horizons, or recruit volunteers! Welcome to BoF!

How to participate?

BoF Hosts registration

If you are willing to be a host at BoF, please send email to organizers@pycon.tw with the information following:

  • Your name
  • Your contact information (mobile, email)
  • The topic you want to discuss or the project, skill, or music you want to share
  • Equipment (Desks and chairs are provided. Please tell us in advance if you need other equipments. We will try our best to help.)
