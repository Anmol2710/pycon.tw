Registration starts from now! This year, BoF is at the end of 2nd conference day (Jun 4).

What is BoF?

Birds of a feather flock together

According to Wikipedia, BoF comes from a motto “Birds of a feather flock together.” A BoF session means an informal meeting at conferences, where the attendees group together based on a shared interest and carry out discussions without any pre-planned agenda.

Last year, PyCon APAC 2015 is also the year Marty McFly (Movie: Back to the Future II) went through time and space to arrive in 2015. PyCon APAC 2015 has decided to use the slogan “Back to __future__ II”, we hope PyCon TW lead us to learn how to use Python to practice at individual ideas, or Python applications in different fields.

Do you want to share ideas and creative projects with PyCon TW? Welcome everyone, to the PyConTW 2016 BoF, Let us implement the future, together!

Any Preparation?

Nope. In BoF, topics are not limited. The only on condition is that the topic has to be related to Python, PyCon, ideas exchanging or point of view discussion, please feel free to participate. Besides, there will be a variety of food!

You can register as a host, bring something new or funny projects: fighting robots, LED control systems, lighting, sound system and etc. Or, you don’t have enough time to give birth to those above, you just simply want to sing a song, Bouncing guitar, no matter how good or bad the product is, or what the crazy noise your music sounds like, you are always welcome to come and share with us!

You can also simply be a passenger, with a curious mind, come to BoF, have chat with others, broaden your horizons, or recruit community volunteers, and so on! Welcome to BoF together!

How to participate?

BoF Hosts registration

If you are willing to be a host at BoF, please send email to organizers@pycon.tw, and please tell us:

  • Your name
  • Your contact information (mobile, email)
  • Name of the topic you want to discuss or the project, skill, music you want to share
  • Equipment (we only provide desks and chairs, if you need additional power, screen and so on, we will try our best to bring them for you. You will have to bring your own if we cannot find those equipment for you.)
