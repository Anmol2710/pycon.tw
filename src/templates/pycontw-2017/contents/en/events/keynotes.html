Carol Willing (willingc)

@WillingCarol
https://willingconsulting.com/

Bio

Carol Willing is a one of the Foundation member in Python Software Foundation, a core developer for Project Jupyter, and a Research Software Engineer at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. She's co-organizes PyLadies San Diego and San Diego Python. an active contributor to open source projects, a maintainer for OpenHatch and the Anita Borg Institute’s open source projects. Combining a love of nature, the arts, and math with a BSE in Electrical Engineering from Duke and an MS in Management from MIT, She enjoys creating and teaching in this field for more than 20 years. Carol Willing has a passion for learning and sharing. Recent lectures include PyCon US 2016, PyData Carolinas 2016, PyCon Philippines 2016.
